About Us

Atal Tinkering Lab, Navyug School Sarojini Nagar

Welcome to our Atal Tinkering Lab at Navyug School Sarojini Nagar! We're all about sparking creativity and curiosity in students. Our lab is like a fun playground where students can turn their cool ideas into real projects in the areas of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM).

What We're About

Our goal is to inspire students to love learning and to get excited about coming up with new ideas. We want them to explore, experiment, and think outside the box. That's why our lab is more than just a classroomâ€”it's a place for students to bring their imaginations to life.

What We Have

  • Cool Tools
    We have awesome tools and gadgets that students can use. From 3D printers to robotics kits, there's a bunch of cool stuff to help students create all sorts of projects.
  • Virtual Studio
    We also have a virtual design studio. It's like a computer space where students can plan and test their ideas using special software. It's a bit like a video game where they can see how things might work before making them for real.
  • Teamwork Areas
    Our lab is set up for teamwork. Students can work together on projects, combining their skills in different subjects. Teamwork makes our projects even better
  • Learning Stuff
    We've got books, online courses, and videos to help students learn more about STEM. So, if they're curious and want to explore on their own, we've got resources to help.

Join the Fun!

Whether you're a student who loves trying new things, a teacher who enjoys guiding students, or a parent excited to see your child learn and create, we invite you to be a part of our exciting journey.

Come and be a part of our community where ideas come to life, and everyone can be an innovator. Let's build a future where challenges are opportunities, and every student can make a difference.

Recent Events

Excitement filled the air at Navyug School Sarojini Nagar as we took part in the Atal Tinkering Marathon, a cool event that lets students showcase their smart projects. Our school submitted a whopping 31 projects, and guess what? Over 90 students joined in on the fun!
The Atal Tinkering Marathon is like a big playground for students who love Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM). It's not just a competition; it's a chance for students to share their clever ideas and work together on awesome projects.
Our projects cover a lot of cool stuffâ€”robotics, electronics, and even ideas to make our world better. What's even more amazing is that so many students got involved. Our Atal Tinkering Lab at Navyug School Sarojini Nagar turned into a buzzing hub of creativity, with students putting their heads together to make cool things happen.
The marathon isn't just about winning; it's about showing off what we can do. Each project tells a unique story of our students' curiosity and their determination to make a difference. We couldn't have done it without our awesome teachers cheering us on.
As our projects joined others from schools all over the country, we realized that we're part of something big. The Atal Tinkering Marathon is like a giant showcase of what students like us can create when given the chance.
Getting ready for the marathon changed us. We learned new things, worked together, and found out just how much we can do. Now, we're waiting to see how our projects did in the marathon. Win or lose, we're proud of what we made and excited about how our ideas might help others.
At Navyug School Sarojini Nagar, we believe in encouraging our students to dream big and be creative. The Atal Tinkering Marathon is a big step in that direction. We're looking forward to seeing what comes next, and we're ready for more adventures in learning and creating! ðŸš€
