About Us

Atal Tinkering Lab, Navyug School Sarojini Nagar

Welcome to our Atal Tinkering Lab at Navyug School Sarojini Nagar! We're all about sparking creativity and curiosity in students. Our lab is like a fun playground where students can turn their cool ideas into real projects in the areas of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM).

What We're About

Our goal is to inspire students to love learning and to get excited about coming up with new ideas. We want them to explore, experiment, and think outside the box. That's why our lab is more than just a classroomâ€”it's a place for students to bring their imaginations to life.

What We Have

  • Cool Tools
    We have awesome tools and gadgets that students can use. From 3D printers to robotics kits, there's a bunch of cool stuff to help students create all sorts of projects.
  • Virtual Studio
    We also have a virtual design studio. It's like a computer space where students can plan and test their ideas using special software. It's a bit like a video game where they can see how things might work before making them for real.
  • Teamwork Areas
    Our lab is set up for teamwork. Students can work together on projects, combining their skills in different subjects. Teamwork makes our projects even better
  • Learning Stuff
    The techers are always there to help and If they're curious and want to explore on their own, we've also got resources to help.

Join the Fun!
